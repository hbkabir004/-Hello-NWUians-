<p>By a short survey in the on the necessary errand of the newcomers and fresher students on their daily and occasional basis, we came to learn about many difficulties to face, for example, admission information (i.e. faculties and departments, course credits, semester fees, waiver, class time etc.) and so on. There is a website of our varsity named ‚Äúnwu.edu.bd‚Äù, but that is mostly used in course registration and showing the result. The students are hardly known to the following information through the website. To provide them with a smooth and handful solution to their necessities, we have come up to the idea of <b>‚ÄúHello, NWUians!(ChatBot)‚Äù<b> which will give the Students not only immediate help but also the feeling of having a friendly and helpful backup on the following occasions.
